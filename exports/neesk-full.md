## Notes

### Backstory

Neesk grew up with her dad and grandparents.

Her grandparents keep telling tales about how their ancestors first came to that plane before the Cracking, and how they helped during that time using an old artifact that was lost.

She never knew her mother and has no memory of her and the rest of her family never wanted to talk about what happened.

Growing up with those tales of missing artifacts and other mysteries, she always loved trying to find mysteries and adventures around her hometown, but she's never been good at it. Her dad never approved of it. He wanted her to train and took the tailor family business after him.

When she got old enough she fled and try to find more information about that possible artifact. But being a changeling led to a lot of people not trusting her, she then mainly used the persona of Laroamae, a half-elf, when she doesn't need to be someone else.

Making a name of herself for her with her small investigation talent she was able to travel a bit on Niumactus.
